## Services restructure (accuracy + compliance)
- REMOVE Solar and Heat Pumps as standalone services — no standalone cards, no standalone pages, no separate advertising. TRC does NOT sell solar or heat pumps on their own and does not want calls for them individually.
- Solar and heat pumps are MEASURES within a retrofit, not services. To pass BER B2, TRC selects ~5–6 measures from a list of ~10 (solar, heat pump, windows, doors, floor insulation, external/internal insulation, HVAC) to reach the highest rating achievable. Fold this into a fleshed-out Retrofitting service.
- The real service offering is: Full Renovations, Extensions, Retrofitting, and Grants (grants reworded — see below).

## Grants wording — COMPLIANCE CRITICAL
- TRC is NOT a One Stop Shop and must NOT be advertised as one. Remove all "One Stop Shop" wording from the site. (There are only eight One Stop Shops in the country; they invested heavily in that status — TRC cannot claim it.)
- What TRC actually does: feeds jobs into a One Stop Shop AGENT who handles the grant process. TRC facilitates, does not hold the status.
- Fiona flagged "we handle the grant paperwork" also needs careful wording — TRC pushes/facilitates it but should not overclaim.
- Preferred framing: the BER improvement journey — taking a house from an E/F/G rating up to B2 (they've taken a 1970s cottage to A1). Frame grants around this energy-upgrade journey.
- TRC also helps clients with COUNCIL grants: derelict property and vacant home grants. Include these.
