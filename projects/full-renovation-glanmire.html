The Story

Keep the house and the street — change everything else.

What the house was. A solid older home on a street the family didn’t want to leave — but cold, dated and awkwardly laid out, with none of the light or warmth a modern family home needs.

What the family needed. To stay put and make the house right: better flow, more light, proper insulation and finishes that would last — without splitting the job across a string of separate trades.

What TRC did. One in-house team handled the whole renovation — reworking the layout, upgrading the fabric, and finishing throughout — with a single point of contact from the first walk-through to the final snag.

The same approach sits behind every full renovation we take on across Cork.
