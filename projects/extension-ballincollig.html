The Story

More room, without giving up the home they had.

Placeholder story — the real details of this project will go here once it’s signed off. [awaiting client]

What the house was. A family home that still suited the family — the location, the neighbours, the schools — but had simply run out of room, with a cramped kitchen and nowhere for everyone to be together.

What the family needed. Space to breathe: a bigger kitchen and family room open to the garden, designed around how they actually live, without the upheaval of moving house.

What TRC did. One in-house team designed and built the extension — foundations, structure, glazing and finishes — and tied it cleanly into the existing house so the new space feels like it was always meant to be there.

It’s the same approach behind every extension we take on across Cork.
