The Story

From cold and costly to warm and low-energy.

Placeholder story — the real details of this project will go here once it’s signed off. [awaiting client]

What the house was. An older Cork home that was cold in every room and expensive to heat, stuck on a low energy rating no matter how high the thermostat went — draughty windows, little insulation and an ageing heating system.

What the family needed. A house that holds its heat and costs far less to run — done as one coordinated job rather than a string of separate measures, with the grant side guided rather than left to them.

What TRC did. One in-house team upgraded the house as a whole — insulation, airtightness, ventilation, windows, solar and a heat pump — bringing it up toward a warm B2, with the grants guided alongside.

It’s the same approach behind every retrofit we take on, with the grants guided as part of the job.
