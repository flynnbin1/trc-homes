What the house was. A traditional Cork terraced home with good bones but a dated, closed-in layout — the kind of older house that needs bringing up to how a family lives today.

What TRC did. We handled the whole renovation in-house — layout, kitchen, living and dining, a new shower room, flooring and finishes throughout — with one team and one point of contact from first walk-through to final snag. The finished house is below.
